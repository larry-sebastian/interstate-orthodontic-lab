Products & Services

Orthodontic Appliances

If your patients are in need of high quality orthodontic appliances, you've come to the right place. Quality guaranteed, at an affordable price.

Removable Appliances

Hawley Retainer

Classic removable retainer with custom acrylic colors and wire configurations.

Spring Aligner

Light-force tooth movement for minor corrections and relapse cases.

T.M.J. Splint

Precision-crafted splints for TMJ disorder treatment and night grinding.

Fixed Appliances

Space Maintainer

Band and loop, distal shoe, and other designs for pediatric spacing.

Lingual Arch

Lower lingual holding arch for maintaining arch length and preventing mesial drift.

Fixed Habit Appliance

Tongue cribs and thumb/finger habit appliances for breaking oral habits.

E-Arch ("Arnold")

Expansion arch for controlled arch development.

R.P.E. / Quad Helix

Rapid palatal expanders and quad helix appliances for maxillary expansion.
